## 🔑 Key Constraint
The Plug AI is currently an **unincorporated community initiative** — not a 501(c)(3). This blocks most foundation grants. **Fiscal sponsorship is the unlock.**

**Fiscal Sponsor Options to Research:**
- [Fractured Atlas](https://www.fracturedatlas.org) — arts/community orgs, ~7% fee
- [Chicago Community Trust](https://cct.org) — local to Chicago, may have fiscal sponsorship program
- [NPEN (Nonprofit Professional Employees Union)](https://www.npen.org) — check
- [Tides Foundation](https://www.tides.org) — national, strong track record
- [Community Shares of Illinois](https://csill.org) — Illinois-specific
